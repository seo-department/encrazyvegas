Highest Payout Slot Machines

Slot payouts determine how much players will receive in real cash when they hit the jackpot over a long period. This payout percentage is also known as the RTP.

For example, if the RTP of a machine is 97%. This means if you wager $1000, then the return should be $970. The remaining 3% is what is known as the house edge. This is the advantage that slots have over players in the long term.

Online casinos will display the RTP along with the gaming provider for the best payout slots. However, there are some factors that may influence the RTP. For instance, the popularity of the slot games. The more players wagering on a particular machine will mean the machine has more big wins.
