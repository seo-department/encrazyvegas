Keno Online Games

There are a number of different online keno games. There is not much difference between each game. The main difference you will notice is the amount of numbers that are drawn, wager amounts, and the amount of numbers you can pick.

For each keno variant, the paytable is shown in the game. This will help give you an indication on what sort of payouts you can expect. It is a good idea to understand these paytables and the rules before you start playing. There are four common keno games.

1. Power Keno

This game uses the same basic rules of classic keno, but there is one additional feature. If you choose the number that is drawn last (as the 20th number), your winnings for the round will be quadrupled.

2. Super Keno

In Super Keno, you can look forward to a payout that is quadrupled if you have the number on your card that is drawn first.

3. Combination Keno

Combination Keno is one of the most versatile keno games. There are multiple ways in which you can win and more betting opportunities. In this variant, you can place bets on groups of numbers.

4. Monkey Keno

In this variant, the setting is completely different. Instead of the traditional drawing of numbers, there are monkeys that throw numbered coconuts on the table.

Keno Terms

Aggregate Limit: the maximum amount you can win in a single game of keno.

Bankroll: The amount of money you have set yourself to play keno.

Balls: The small numbered balls used in keno to determine the winning combination.

Bubble: The traditional container used to store the numbered balls.

Call: The act of calling out the number that has been drawn.

Catch Zero: A bet that not numbers will be marked off. Only some keno games offer this bet.

Deuce: Two keno numbers found next to each other that you have marked off.

Numbered Pool: The whole series of numbers from which keno numbers are drawn. This typically ranges between 1 and 80.

Straight Ticket: A standard keno ticket you can use for live keno and online keno.

Split Ticket: A keno ticket that has more than one set of keno numbers and played in one round.
