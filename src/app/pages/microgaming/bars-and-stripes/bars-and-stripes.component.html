Bars and Stripes Slot Features

The Bars and Stripes slot background is designed to look like the gameplay screen is set against the American flag. The minimum coin value is 0.01, making it a great choice for players of all budgets. The game has 2 scatter symbols, of which one triggers the bonus features.

Pick a Game Bonus Game

The hats are an icon you want to watch out for. Landing three hats in a row will trigger the bonus feature. Upon activation of the bonus, you will hear a drum roll and be presented with five hats. You will have to choose three of them to reveal your prize. During this bonus, you can win up to 47,500 coins.

Bars and Stripes Logo

The Bars & Stripes Logo is the game’s Wild Symbol. The Wild can help complete winning combinations by substituting for other symbols, except the scatter. All winning combinations with the wild present will double your payouts.

American Football

The Scatter Symbol is the American Football. The scatter symbol pays both left to right and across the pay-line. All wins with the scatter are multiplied by the total bet and are added to the existing pay-line wins.
