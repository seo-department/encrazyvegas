Mega Moolah Slot Features

Mega Moolah is a Monstrous Mating of Africa, and Moolah can only promise you the opportunity of hitting the jackpot big time. The Lion is wilder than ever, as he devours all other symbols which he substitutes for. He can take the place of all symbols except the scattered Monkey.

Free Spins

You can activate up to 15 free spins by landing three scatter icons. All wins during the free spins round also come with a 3x multiplier. You can also retrigger the free spins rounds.

Progressive Jackpot

The progressive jackpot feature is one that is randomly triggered. If you bet the maximum on each line for each spin, then you could stand an even higher chance of making more Moolah. Once triggered, you will be taken to the Mega Moolah jackpot wheel. Each section of the wheel is linked to one of the four-tiered jackpots. You will spin the wheel to reveal your prize!

The jackpot wheel has four colors. Each color represents a different jackpot; Mega, Major, Minor, and Mini Progressives. These are represented as follows:

  • One white space which is the Mega Progressive.
  • Three yellow slices (Major Progressive).
  • Six orange slices (Minor Progressive).
  • Ten red slices (Mini Progressive).

Mega Moolah Symbols

When you launch the game, you will find a game screen with 5-reel and 25 paylines. The gameplay is set in the African Savannah with various native animal symbols. These include Buffalo, Ibis, Giraffe, and Zebra. These are accompanied by the regular playing card icons of 10, J, K, Q, and A.

The Lion

The Lion serves as the game’s wild symbol. It can substitute for all other symbols on the reels to help build winning combinations. Wins with the wild symbol will also come with a 2x multiplier. In addition, successive lion wild symbols will award a prize of their own. The biggest prize will come when you land 5 wilds on an active pay line, giving you a 15,000x multiplier payout.

The Monkey

The monkey is another valuable symbol you will want to find on your screen. Acting as the games scatter icon, it can payout in any position. You will find him swinging from tree to tree, giving out instant payouts between 2 and 100x your bet. In addition, if you get three or more monkeys sitting on your screen, it will trigger 15 free spins. Each win during the free spins round will come with a 3x multiplier. The free spins feature is also recursive.
