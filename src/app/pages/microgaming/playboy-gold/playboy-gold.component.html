Playboy Gold: Bonus Features

Before you can spin the reels to stand a chance of triggering the bonus rounds, you will need to place your bets. You can adjust your coin size and coin bet amount, or you can just press the bet max to place the highest bet amount.

Jumbo Block Feature

One of the first features you are most likely to come across. The Jumbo Blocks, as the name suggests, are massive symbols featuring the Playmates Stephanie, Carly, Gia, and Hiromi. When you land of these on the reels in full, one of three things can happen:

  • You will receive an instant prize
  • You will receive a multiplier prize if there are any winning combinations on the reels.
  • The re-spins feature will be activated.

Re-spins Bonus

This feature is activated in the Jumbo Block bonus. When you trigger the re-spins bonus, the Jumbo Blocks will expand from 4x6 to 4x10 and will cover the four middle reels for either two, three, or four spins. Also, any wilds that appear on reels 1 and 6 will become sticky.

Wheel Bonus Feature

The wheel bonus is activated when at least three scatters appear anywhere on the reels. This will give you a chance to spin the wheel one time. Prizes include multiplier payouts between 20x and 100x your bet. You can also trigger free spins or a match bonus game.

Free Spins Feature

If you land on the keys to the Playboy mansion during the wheel bonus game, you will trigger the free spins rounds. During the free spins, reels 1 and 6 will have sticky wilds, plus you are guaranteed a full Jumbo Block with every free spin.

Match Bonus

The wheel bonus can also give you a match bonus round if you land on the Playboy Bunnies. You will select cards from a row in this feature until you can match a pair of Playboy Bunnies. There are different payouts for each pair of Playboy Bunnies. To make this feature even more exciting, if you can match a pair of girls on the first two flips, you will receive all of the prizes combined. Every flip thereafter, the prize is multiplied. A 10x bonus will be rewarded for a flip on the 7th match.

Playboy Cufflink

The Playboy cufflink represents the game’s wild symbol. This symbol can replace all other icons on the reels except for the scatter. It is also your special icon to trigger the bonus rounds.
