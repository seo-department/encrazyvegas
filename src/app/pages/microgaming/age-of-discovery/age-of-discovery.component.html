Treasure Hunt Bonus Game

The treasure hunt bonus game activates when the compass symbol appears on reels 2, 3, or 5. ‘X’ marks the spot in this bonus game. You can select up to 28 of the 30 crosses on the map, giving you a chance to win up to 32,250 coins!

Symbols

The payout of winning combinations is calculated by multiplying the coin value by the number of coins wagered and then by the multiplier for the combination. The multipliers for the combinations of three, four, and five symbols appearing on a single payline are as follows:

  • A sailing vessel – 30, 200 and 1,000
  • A coat of arms – 15, 180 and 800
  • A sextant – 10, 160 and 600
  • Papaya – 8, 100 and 200
  • Guava – 6, 80 and 175
  • Mango – 5, 50 and 150
  • Avocado – 5, 30 and 100
  • A banana is the only regular symbol that forms a combination of as few as two symbols – 2, 4, 20 and 50

Gold Coin

The gold coin acts as the wild symbol. The wild can substitute for all other symbols except for the scatter and the bonus symbols. As it substitutes for most of the symbols on the reels, it will help you create more winning combinations.

Sea Snake

The sea snake is the Age of Discovery scatter symbol. A scatter symbol holds the power of a bonus multiplier that can land you a larger payout. The scatter symbol wins are multiplied by all bets placed and are added to the pay-line base wins. These multipliers can be 5x, 20x, or 80x your original wager.
