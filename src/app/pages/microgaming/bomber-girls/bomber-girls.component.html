Bomber Girls Features

There is plenty to keep yourself entertained with the Bomber Girls slot machine. The war-theme slot is represented with authentic sound effects and symbols. There are also some real special effects that will let you win some serious real money payouts!

Free Spins

Landing at least three compass symbols on the middle 3 reels will activate the free spins. The first two compasses are added together to get the number of free spins you will get, and the third compass will indicate the multiplier win amount. There are up to 14 free spins you can get with a multiplier worth up to 3 times your bet.

High-Low Bonus Game

The High-Low Bonus activates when you land bonus symbols on the outer reels simultaneously. In this bonus, one card will be displayed to you face-up, and four cards face down. The aim will be to predict whether the next card is either higher or lower than the previous one.

Double Up Bonus Feature

This bonus activates when you guess all four cards correctly during the high-low bonus round. In this bonus, you will receive 2 cards face-down. You will have the choice to either collect, bet half or all of your bonus winnings for a chance to double your payout. The aim will be to guess one last time whether the second card will be higher or lower than the first one.

Bomber Girls Logo

The Bomber Girl symbol will give you the game’s highest payout when you land five of them on an active payline. It also represents the game’s wild symbol and can substitute for all of the girls on the reels to form winning combinations. Any win with the wild present will earn you a double payout.

Compass

The compass is the game’s scatter symbol, and only appears on reels 2, 3, and 4. These symbols are what you need to land to trigger the free spins bonus.

Bonus Symbol

The bonus symbol has a whiskey glass with playing cards and chips. Land this icon on reels one and five together to trigger the high-low bonus. If you manage to guess all four of those cards correctly, it will open the second bonus round, where you have a chance to increase your winnings even more!
