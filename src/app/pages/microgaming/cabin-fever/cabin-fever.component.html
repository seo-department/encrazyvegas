Cabin Fever Features

Cabin Fever is a 5-reel, 20 paylines, 10-coin game with wilds, scatters, and great bonus rounds.

Free Spins

When the Sun icon appears on reels 1 and 5, you will activate the free spins round. You will receive 20 free spins, and all wins will have a double payout. The free spins feature can be re-triggered. All bets and paylines will remain the same as at the start of the free spins round.

Gamble Feature

Unlock the gamble feature with any winning combination. Click the gable button to activate the feature. You will be presented with one face down card where you will have to guess Red or Black. Guess the color correctly, and you double your winnings. You can keep guessing correctly until the gamble limit is reached. You want to leave the bonus round; you can collect your winnings and return to the game.

Symbols

All the snow covered symbols suit the theme of the game. The high paying symbols are made up of the bear family, and the fruit icons are the low paying symbols. Below are the icons you will find and the maximum payout for each when you place the max bet.

  • Daddy Bear – 300-coins
  • Mamma Bear – 250-coins
  • Young Bears – 250-coins
  • Baby Bear – 200-coins
  • Oranges – 150-coins
  • Watermelon – 120-coins
  • Lemons – 100-coins
  • Plum – 100-coins
  • Cherries – 60-coins

Wild

The wild logo is the game’s wild symbol that can substitute for all other icons on the reels to help form a winning combination. However, the wild cannot replace a scatter or trigger symbol. The highest payout for landing 5 wild symbols on an active payline is 5,000-coins when you wager the maximum.  

Squirrel

The Squirrel icon in the game is the scatter symbol. This scatter will reward you 200-coins when five of them appear anywhere on the reels. They also award multiplied payouts, and all wins are added to payline wins.  

Sun

The sun symbol represents the trigger symbol. When the sun shines on reels 1 and 5, it will trigger 20 free spins.
