Asian Beauty Slot Features

Asian Beauty is an elegant video slot game with ambiance music. However, you have the option to customize your gameplay experience. There are scatter payouts, a wild, and a bonus game.

Asian Beauty Mirror Bonus

This bonus game triggers randomly, so there is no combination you need to land on the reels to trigger it. The game challenges you to win up to 9,000-coins. The game will display 12 mirrors; you will select each one to reveal an Asian Beauty. If you match three beauties, you will win the payout for that beauty. The payout can be anything up to 30 times your bet amount.

Free Spins

Landing three or more scatter symbols will trigger the Asian Beauty free spins. You will receive up to 25 free spins, and all winnings during the free spins round will be doubled. The free spins rounds are also recursive. Your bet amount for the free spins rounds will remain the same as the start of the round.

Asian Beauty Symbols

The game features four Asian women surrounded by other symbols such as Plum Trees, Wooden Boxes, Lotus Flowers, Silver Cups, and Golden Cups.

Asian Beauty Logo

This logo can substitute for all other symbols on the reels except for the scatter.  

Jewelry Box

The Jewelry Box scatter is your crucial symbol to trigger the free spins round. Scatter wins pay in any position, and all wins come with a multiplier. Wins with 3 scatters will multiply your win by 5x, 4 scatters will multiply your win by 15 times you bet amount, and 5 scatters will multiply your bet amount by 100 times. Landing 3, 4, or 5 scatters will trigger 10, 15, or 25 free spins, respectively.
