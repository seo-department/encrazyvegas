Arctic Fortune Slot Features

Arctic Fortune is a 5-reel video slot game that has no fixed paylines. Instead, there are 1024 ways to win. There are also great bonus features that make the game even more entertaining.

Shoot the Spiders

When you land, three, four, or five scatters will activate this unique bonus. Upon activation, you will have to use a bow and arrow to kill as many giant spiders as possible. This will reward you with up to 40 free spins, multipliers as much as 6x, and up to 9,000-coins.

Free Spins

You win up to 40 free spins during the shoot the spiders feature. When you shoot a spider, it will reveal how many free spins you get. The free spins are also recursive.

Arctic Fortune Logo

The arctic fortune game logo is the game’s wild symbol. Wilds are used to creating bigger and more winning combinations, more often. Wild symbols do this by replacing any non-winning symbols with a symbol that can create a winning combination. The only symbol it cannot replace is the scatter symbol, which is another special symbol. Wilds in this online slot game only appear on the second and fourth reels.

Treasure Map

The treasure map icon is the scatter symbol. These can create their own winning combinations by landing two of them on the reels for an even money payout. However, the real fun begins when you land three or more scatters. Landing three or more scatters will activate the bonus round, where you can be awarded up to 40 free spins with a 6x multiplier. After the free spins feature, all your coins will be calculated and shown to you. The free spins feature can also be retriggered during the free spins bonus game.
