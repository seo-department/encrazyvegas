Features

The theme of Bush Telegraph is an African cartoon themed slot game set in the African Jungle. The bonus features of the game include free spins, a bongo drums bonus, a gamble feature.

Bongo Drums Bonus

The appearance of 3, 4, or 5 of the Bongo Bonus symbols on the reels activate the Bongo Drums bonus. In this feature, you can win up to 2,500-coins. Upon activation, you will choose among the 12 Hidden Drums to reveal your bonus win amount. The more bonus symbols appearing on the reels, the more drums you can choose. 3 symbols will let you choose 3 times, 4 symbols will let you choose 4 drums, and 5 symbols will let you pick 5. In this feature, you will win the total of the bonus wins revealed.

Free Spins

To activate the 10 free spins rounds, you will need to land at least three Seedpod symbols. All wins during the free spins rounds will come with a triple payout. The bet amount and payouts will remain the same as at the amount when the feature was activated. The free spins are re-cursive. Scattered wins are also added to payline wins.

Gamble Feature

After any winning combination appearing on the reels, you will be eligible to increase your winnings with the gamble feature. To activate the bonus, press the gamble button on the screen. To double your winnings, you will need to guess the face-down card's color on the screen correctly. If you guess incorrectly, then the round will end. You can continue correctly guessing until the limit is reached. To leave the round, you can save your winnings by pressing the collect button.
