Free Spins

The free spins bonus is activated when you land three, four, or five scatter symbols. This will give you free 15 free spins, and will wins during the free spins round will come with a 3x multiplier. You may also retrigger the free spins round by landing at least three scatters on a free spin.  

Gamble Feature

With any winning combination you land, you will have the chance to either double or quadruple your winnings. After each win, press the gamble button to activate the feature. During the feature, you will be presented with one card. You will aim to correctly guess the color of the card (Red / Black) to double your winnings or the suit of card (Hearts, Diamonds, Clubs, or Spade) to quadruple your winnings. You can continue to double your winnings until the gamble limit is reached.  

Symbols

The cartoonish style symbols found in Adventure Palace are brightly colored to suit the tropical jungle theme. The high paying symbols are made up of Monkeys, Tigers, Peacocks, Snakes, and Pelicans. The low paying symbols are your traditional playing card symbols 9, 10, A, J, K, and Q that are carved out of relics with vines wrapped around them to give them the jungle look and feel.

Elephant

The Elephant Symbol is the game’s wild. It can substitute for all other symbols on the reels except for the scatter. When the wild icon forms part of a winning combination, the payout will be doubled. Landing five wild elephant icons will give you the highest payout of 10,000-coins!.

Hidden Palace

The hidden palace within the jungle is the game’s scatter icon. If 3, 4, or 5 scatters land or appear simultaneously on the reels, it will activate the free spins feature. Upon activation, you will receive 15 free spins. Every win achieved during the free spins feature will be tripled. All scatter wins will also add to payline wins and are multiplied by the total bet.
