Features

Buffet Bonanza is a 5 reel slot game with 25 paylines. The bet limits cater to all players, with bets ranging between 0.01 and 100.00. The maximum jackpot is 6,000-coins for landing 5 wild symbols. However, you can increase this amount with the bonus features.

Free Spins

When three or more buffet scatters appear anywhere on the reels, it will activate 10 free spins. All wins during the free spins rounds will come with a 2x multiplier. During the free spins, you will also get random wilds on the second and fourth reels. At least one wild will also be added each round. The free spins feature is also recursive.

Buffet Bonus

When the Diner wild symbol appears on the third reel, the Buffet feature is activated. In this unique bonus round, the diner symbol will fill the third reel and act as a wild. Reels 1, 2, 4, and 5. Will then be re-spun. You are not required to place any additional bets for this spin. You will then see pies being thrown onto the reels, and any symbol that they hit will be turned into a wild. Once the reels stop spinning and no more pies are thrown onto the reels, the wins will be calculated. The feature cannot be retriggered during the free spins rounds.

Gamble Feature

After every win, there is a Gamble option whereby you can take your existing winnings and play to double or quadruple them. Bet on the correct card color to increase your current winnings by a multiplier of 2, and bet on the correct card suit to increase your winnings by 4 times.

Diner

The wild symbol is the Diner symbol. The wild will help you complete winning combinations by substituting all other symbols in the game except the scatter symbol. The wild symbol can also be stacked across the reels.

Buffet

The Buffet logo stands as the scatter symbol of this game.  The scatter will award you with payouts whenever it appears at least twice anywhere on the reels. Three scatter symbols appearing on the reels trigger the 10 free spins, which doubles the payouts for winning combinations when they are active. If the Buffet bonus and the free spins feature is triggered during the same spin, the bonus feature is played out first.
