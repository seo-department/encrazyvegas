Brief History of Video Poker

The origin of video poker dates back to the 1800s. It draws on Chares Fey’s first slot machine, the liberty bell in 1898. Despite the inspiration behind video poker, the game never really made headlines until the 1970s. This was when the first electronic video poker machine was introduced.

  • 1975: Fortune Bell Company releases the first electronic slot machine.
  • 1979: Si Redd’s Coin Machines introduces their version of Draw Poker.
  • 1980’s: New video poker games were introduced.
  • 1980’s: House edge was introduced to video poker machines.
  • 1990’s: The most significant change in video poker with the introduction of Internet video poker. Video poker online has given new ways for different types of video poker games
