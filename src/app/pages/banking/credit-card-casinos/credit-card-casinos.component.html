VISA was introduced in 1958 and today, VISA credit cards account for a majority of the market share at approximately just over 50%. VISA cards are internationally accepted in over 200 countries and publicly traded on the New York Stock Exchange.

MasterCard was first introduced in 1966 and today, the company has an estimated 31% of the market share. It is accepted in 210 countries with over 25 000 financial institutions across the globe issuing their brand.

Discover

Discover was founded in 1985. Today, the company has roughly 8% of the market share and is accepted in 50 countries. They are well-known for offering clients cash-back bonuses for using their Discover card.

American Express

American Express, otherwise known as Amex was founded in 1850. It is one of the most reputable credit card companies and is accepted in 160 countries.  This credit card issuer is known for their excellent support. However, American Express requires clients to pay annual membership fees.
