Pass line bet

The pass line bet is the most basic of bets in the game of craps. This bet requires that players roll either a 7 or 11 to win on the come-out roll. However, the pass line bet loses if the dice roll is a 2 or 3. Any other number becomes the point. The point must then be rolled before rolling a 7.

Don’t pass line bet

The don’t pass line bet is the exact opposite of the pass line bet. The only difference here is that players need to roll a 2, 3 or 12 to win. That means that rolling a 7 or 11 causes all bets on the don’t pass line to lose. If a point is established, a 7 must be rolled before rolling the point again.

List of Bets in Craps

Craps bets offer a wider variety of bets than almost any other casino game. Now that you know the basic bets of the craps game, here is a list of all the other bets you can make:

  • Come Bets: A bet that you can only place after the point is established. Rolling a 7 or 11 after placing a come bet means you win. However, if the dice role is a 2, 3 or 12, you lose. Rolling any other number makes this your point number for the come bet.
  • Don’t Come Bets: These bets lose if the dice role is a 7 or 11 but win if the dice role is a 2 or 3. Rolling anything else establishes the point. You can only win during a point if you roll a 7 before rolling the point.
  • Place Bets: Place bets wager on a specific number from the range between 4 and 10. Rolling the number chosen before rolling a 7 results in a win. You can also use a place bet to wager on rolling a 7 before rolling a number between 4 and 10 which you choose.
  • Buy Bets: Buy bets are the same as place bets, but with a higher payout and a 5% commission.
  • Lay Bets: This is the opposite of the buy bet in that players need to roll a 7 before their number. This bet also comes with a 5% commission.
  • Big 6 or 8: A bet that any 6 or 8 will appear before a 7.
  • Hard-ways: These bets require players to roll a double before rolling a 7.
  • Field Bets: With this bet if the dice role is a 2, 3, 4, 9, 10, 11, or 12 you win. If the dice role is a 5,6, 7, or 8 you lose.
  • Proposition Bets: There are several types of proposition bets. Players win this bet by choosing and rolling the following outcomes: Any 7, Any 11, Any Craps, Any Deuce, Aces, or Boxcar.

  • Stick to the pass line: These bets are the best for beginners and offer a decent house edge. The pass line is also not so difficult to learn and can win you some cash in real money craps.
