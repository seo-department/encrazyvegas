Video Poker Tip 1 – Understand the Game

There are a number of different video poker variants for players to enjoy online. Each has its own set of rules and payouts. It is important to learn the rules of the variant you are playing. Not being able to identify even just a small difference may cost players a potential win.

Video Poker Tip 2 – Wager the Maximum

Like with any casino game, players will want to try win as much as they can. One tip to do this in video poker is by placing the max bet. This is also the only way players can pocket the highest jackpot. Players will not want to land a royal flush at the end of the round without being able to walk away with the full jackpot prize.

Video Poker Tip 3 – Set a Time Limit

Video poker is not only about luck. There is also some strategy involved. That is why we suggest players set a time limit. It is not worth playing if you are unable to concentrate. Players need to keep a fresh mind to make the best-informed decisions possible.

Video Poker Tip 4 – Look at the Pay Tables

Not all video poker variants offer the same pay outs. It is in the player’s best interest to search through the different variants and find games with the most profitable payout tables.

Video Poker Tip 5 - Control the Pace of the Game

Video poker is a great game for players who want to take their time to make the best possible decision. This is known as the “slow-down strategy” in Jacks or Better. There is no one around to keep a stopwatch on you. So, take your time to keep the best cards.

Video Poker Tip 6 – Play Progressive Video Poker Machines

Progressive video poker machines give players the best chance of reaping in the most rewards from small bets.

Video Poker Tip 7 – Play for Free

Free video poker allows players to practice as much as they need. For players who are only looking to have fun, playing for free is the way to go. There are no risks involved or time limits. Players can practice for as long as they need to. It is a great way to try out different strategies and improve your overall video poker game.

Video Poker Tip 8 – Keep Track of Bankroll

Playing real money video poker can be extremely fun, especially when players find themselves on a winning streak. However, it is very important to keep track of the bankroll. Players can easily get caught up in the game and spend more than they budgeted for.
