Double Bonus Poker Trainer

Double bonus poker is a fun video poker variation that has a higher variance than jacks or better. To make up for the higher variance, double bonus poker offers a bigger payout for some of the four-of-a-kind hands. But, this does come at a reduced payout for two pairs of even money, stead of a payout worth 2:1. The game also features a small house edge making it one of the best video poker choices.

In this guide, we will give you information for the perfect strategy to gain a small edge over the house.

How to Play Double Bonus Poker

Those of you who know how to play video poker will make an easy transition to double bonus poker. The aim is to look at the paytable and get a hand you see there. Below is how you can play double bonus poker in four easy steps.

  • Place your bet.
  • Press deal to receive your first five cards.
  • Select the cards you want to hold and which ones you want to replace.
  • Press draw to receive new cards for the ones you have discarded.

There is also a double your money game after every win. To activate this feature, after any win, you press the double button. It will present you with five: one card face-up and four face-down. The aim of the bonus game is to select one card you think will have a higher value than the face-up card. It does, you double your winnings. If you, you lose it all.
