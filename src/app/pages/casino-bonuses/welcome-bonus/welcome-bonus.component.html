Types of Casino Welcome Bonuses

There are a number of different types of casino bonuses on offer. The most common one offered is a match deposit.

Cash Match Bonus

This is the most popular casino bonus. Most casinos will advertise free money. For example, claim $1000 free. By using the term free, players think they are getting free money.

The catch, in most cases, is that players have to make a certain deposit to claim the $1000 bonus. With these bonuses, the more your deposit, the more you are likely to get back in return.

Match Deposit Bonus

Some casinos may put a max limit on the bonus amount you can claim. These types of bonuses are known as match deposit bonuses.

For example, 100% up to $1000. This means if you deposit $800, you will receive $800 in bonus funds. However, if you deposit $1200, you will receive $1000.
