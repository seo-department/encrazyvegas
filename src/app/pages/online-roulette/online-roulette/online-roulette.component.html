Online Roulette Outside Bets

These are roulette bets placed on the outside of the roulette table. These bets are represented in green and contain groups of bets. They include:

  • Column bets (Groups of 12)
  • Odd / Even bets
  • Red / Black bets
  • Bets on numbers 1-18 or 19-36

Online Roulette Inside Bets

Inside bets are more complex compared to outside bets. These bets are represented on the inside of the roulette table and cover numbers 1 to 36. These numbers alternate between red and black colours. These colours correspond with the roulette wheel. There are a number of different inside bets for players to choose from.

  • Straight Up Bets: These are bets placed on single numbers.
  • Corner Bets: One bet covering fur numbers on the roulette wheel. If the ball lands on any of the four numbers covered, the bets win.
  • Line Bets: A single bet covering two rows with three numbers in each row. If the ball lands on any of these six numbers, the player wins.
  • Neighbour Bets: This is a five series straight-up bet. For example, placing a bet on 1 number and four of its neighbouring numbers.

Popular Roulette Online USA Terminology

  • Biased numbers: A term used in roulette to refer to numbers on the wheel that the ball has been statistically proven to land on more often than others.
  • Column bet: A bet placed on 12 different numbers in one of the three columns of 12 numbers on the roulette table.
  • Corner bet: A single bet placed on one of the four numbers touching each other on the table layout.
  • Croupier: Also referred to as the dealer. The croupier is responsible for taking bets.
  • Dozen bet: This is a wager placed on a group of 12 numbers. This can be either 1-12, 13-24 or 25-36.
  • High bet: A wager that the ball will land in a pocket numbered between 19 and 36.
  • Inside bet: These bets are much harder to hit but hold a greater reward if you do. This bet covers individual and a small group of numbers.
  • Outside bet: These are bets placed on the outer layout of the roulette table. It covers a larger section of possible outcomes, but pays out less.
  • Straight-up bet: Feeling lucky? This bet is placed on a single number on the roulette table. Should your number be the one the ball lands on, your bet wins 35:1.
