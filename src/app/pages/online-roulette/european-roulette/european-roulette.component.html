European Roulette Table & Wheel

The European roulette table has 37 numbers starting with zero then 1 to 36. The numbers 1 to 36 are divided into red and black numbers. Each of the numbers face towards the inner side of the roulette wheel.

To the right of the zero there are nine small numbers in black (2, 4, 6, 8, 10, 11, 13, 15, 17), and then nine big numbers in red (19, 21, 23, 25, 27, 32, 34, 36). To the left there are nine numbers in black, (20, 22, 26, 28, 29, 31, 33, 35) and then there are nine numbers in red, (1, 3, 5, 7, 9, 12, 16, 18).

Betting Types

There are five types of inside and outside you can place in European roulette. Outside bets are recommended for beginner players as they are easier to understand and they are less risky. These bets are also much easier to win with but the payouts are much smaller. For more experienced players, there are inside bets. These bets are much harder to win with, but they payouts are worth it.

Call Bets

Besides your regular betting options, European roulette offers calls bets. There are three main you can make: Voisin du zero, Tiers du Cyclindre and Orphelin.

Voisin du zero is a bet placed on numbers which are neighbours of zero. Tiers du cyclindre are bets on numbers which are opposite to the numbers chosen during voisin du zero. Orphelin call bets are bets on the remaining eight numbers on the wheel.
