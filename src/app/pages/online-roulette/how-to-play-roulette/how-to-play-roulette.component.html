Types of Roulette Betting Explained

Inside Bets: Bets on individual numbers or a combination of numbers.

Straight Up bets: Wagers on individual numbers.

Split Bets: These wagers cover 2 numbers on the roulette table.

Street Bets or Line Bets: Bets are placed on the vertical line separating the inside and outside betting areas.

Corner Bets: These wagers cover four numbers on the roulette table. They can be placed placing betting chips in the corner so that it is touching all the four corners of the numbers.

Basket Bet: This bet covers 5 numbers, 00-3.

Double Street Bet: This is a wager that covers 6 numbers and can be wagered on by players placing their chip on the line separating the outside and inside areas.

Outside Bets: Wagers placed in the boxes surrounding the numbers.

Red and Black Bets: Bets on the roulette ball dropping on either a black or red number. In these bets, only the colour is relevant.

Even or Odd Bets: Wagers on the ball falling on either an even or odd number.

Dozens Bet: Wagers on 12 consecutive numbers, either; 1-12; 13-24; 25-36.

Column Bets: Wagers placed on 12 numbers, which are arranged in vertical columns. These wagers can be made by players placing their chips at the bottom of the layout.
