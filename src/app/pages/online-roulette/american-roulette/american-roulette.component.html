American Roulette Strategy to Win

There are a number of strategies and systems you can use while playing American roulette. However, these strategies are structured ways of either increasing or decreasing your bets based on the previous rounds results. The most popular system is the Martingale system.

How this strategy works is that each time you lose, you will double you bet amount for the next round. When you win, you will keep the same bet. For example, if you placed a bet of $15 and lose, your next bet will be $30. If you lose again your bet will be $60. If you win, you will keep your bet at $60.
